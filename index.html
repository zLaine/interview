Interview

  • High Concept: Players through an interview, after choosing stats for themselves to have focused on during their education.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will be prompted to think about their own strengths and weaknesses and get to tinker around with interviewing.
  • Genre: A text-based "choose your own adventure"-esque story involving stats.
  • Game play: The player uses to mouse to click and choose what they want to spend their skill points on, then navigate choices in the interview.
  • Prototype goal: Experimentation with making a game so relient on text enjoyable, as well as figuring out button use and better text handling.
  • Features: The specific context is what makes this game unique -- an interview specific to game programmers.
  • Setting/Premise: It's about a person getting out of school and interviewing.
  • Target Audience: People who would like to get a job programming games, or enjoy text-based games.
  • Play time: As it currently stands, this should probably take only a few minutes.
  • Strategies: Effective strategies in the current version involve insuring that you're versatile -- having no skills in any particular area can really hurt your chances of getting a job!
